Future apps with Angular2 and companions: Ionic2 + Firebase

by Maxim Salnikov @webmaxru

Do you want to look into the future? How will we create web and mobile applications in 2016? Well, it's possible even without inventing time machine. Let's just take alpha/beta-versions of the most promising frameworks, add some magic and cook the app. The reference app for all the developers. Web or mobile - who cares, there will be only fine line between these in 2016. Sounds ambitious? 100%! But we love ventures and being on cutting edge of web technologies, so let's go for it!
