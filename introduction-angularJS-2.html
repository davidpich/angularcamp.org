About Nir Kaufman

Nir is the Head of AngularJS Development of 500Tech - a leading AngularJS consultancy in Israel. He has been developing software for more than a decade, and he is passionate Full-Stack JavaScript developer and an AngularJS expert.

Nir played a major role in AngularJS teams in many companies and startups in both Israel and abroad as an architect , developer and consultant.

As a community activist, Nir is the organizer of the AngularJS-IL meetup group (with more than 2700 members), and a regular speaker at conferences and technology events such as: Extreme DevCon, Ng-Conf IL, AngularJS-IL, Expert Days week 2014/2015, JS-IL to name just a few. he is currently writing his first Angular2 book (packt publishing)
