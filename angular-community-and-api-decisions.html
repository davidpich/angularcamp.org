Angular Community and API decisions

by David Rodenas @drpicox

I’ve been collaborating with Angular team in the 1.5 as a contributor and I have been participating and seen how the new module.component feature is getting defined.
I’ll present few examples of some aspects of the definition and how it is taking form step by step driven by community.
I want to show how community, any one, can effectively drive API improvements through Github.



About David Rodenas

David Ródenas is PhD in Computer Sciences and also a Computer Engineer.
He is working with Angular for almost 3 years, 18 years developing webs apps, his first web App was a Tamagochi in 1998 (working in Netscape and Mosaic).
Currently he is a FrontEnd Architect working in tens of projects and often develop the most complex or critical tasks, specially when performance matters.
He has been a teacher for more than 8 years and he is making talks for more than 10 years. His motif to encourage people to do talks is "If you have discovered something cool, go and present it".

About

AngularCamps are a series of community run events around the world. Launched in Barcelona, Spain, the goal of the camps is to help strengthen the skills of the Angular community and offer insights to techniques, trends and feature development.
