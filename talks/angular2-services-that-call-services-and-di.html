Angular2: Services that call services and DI

by David Rodenas @drpicox

We are in Angular2 beta0 it is time to start learning Angular2. I have reviewed lots of examples available in the web and practically none shows what happens if you have Services that uses Services, and none is explaining how to handle large projects with multiple dependencies and complex DI. In this talk I’ll present how to make Services that uses other Services, but also how to pack functionalities in like angular1 modules that can be composed to create larger apps without messing with injectors.



About David Rodenas

David Ródenas is PhD in Computer Sciences and also a Computer Engineer.
He is working with Angular for almost 3 years, 18 years developing webs apps, his first web App was a Tamagochi in 1998 (working in Netscape and Mosaic).
Currently he is a FrontEnd Architect working in tens of projects and often develop the most complex or critical tasks, specially when performance matters.
He has been a teacher for more than 8 years and he is making talks for more than 10 years. His motif to encourage people to do talks is "If you have discovered something cool, go and present it".
