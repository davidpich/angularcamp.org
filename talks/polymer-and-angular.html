About Jorge del Casar

Also, I'm siesta-time speaker, my talks are usually after lunch to avoid people sleep during the conference bewcuse I'm very energetic. I hope we can talk and share interesting conversation and pieces of code. About

AngularCamps are a series of community run events around the world. Launched in Barcelona, Spain, the goal of the camps is to help strengthen the skills of the Angular community and offer insights to techniques, trends and feature development.
